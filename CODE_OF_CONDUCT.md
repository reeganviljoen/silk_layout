# Code of Conduct

SilkLayout should be a respectful, useful place to collaborate.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and specific.
- Assume good intent, especially around rendering differences and bug reports.
- Keep feedback focused on code, behavior, documentation, and tests.
- Make room for people with different experience levels.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated disruption of issues, pull requests, or discussions.

## Reporting

If you see unacceptable behavior, contact the repository owner privately. Reports
will be handled as promptly and discreetly as possible.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block users, or take other
reasonable action to keep the project healthy.
